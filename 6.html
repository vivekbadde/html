Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled egg.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1ts butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the wggs and cream in a bowl.
  3. Once the butter has melted, add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centreevert 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
